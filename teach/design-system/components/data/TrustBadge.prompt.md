Marketing trust pill — emoji/icon + bold label. Used in the homepage trust row.

```jsx
<TrustBadge icon="🇩🇪" label="Made in Germany" />
<TrustBadge icon="🔒" label="GDPR Compliant" />
<TrustBadge icon="⚡" label="MCP Protocol" accent />
```

`accent` gives the purple-tinted standout treatment (reserved for the MCP/brand badge).
